# External Integrations & Connections

## What Are Integrations?

Integrations are **connections to other services** that help iPerform work with the tools you already use. Think of them as bridges that let different systems talk to each other automatically.

**Why They Matter**:
- Automate data flow (no manual copying)
- Reduce errors (no retyping)
- Save time (instant updates)
- Connect your business tools
- Provide better features

## Current Integrations

### 1. Facebook Lead Ads Integration

**What It Does**:
Automatically brings leads from your Facebook ad campaigns directly into iPerform.

**How It Works** (Simple View):
1. Someone fills out your Facebook ad form
2. Facebook sends the information to iPerform instantly
3. Lead appears in your system within seconds
4. Salesperson gets notified
5. Follow-up begins

**What You Get**:
- **Instant Lead Capture**: No delay, no manual entry
- **Complete Information**: All form fields transferred
- **Source Tracking**: Know which ad brought the lead
- **Campaign Attribution**: Measure ad performance
- **ROI Visibility**: See which ads are worth the money

**Setup Requirements**:
- Facebook Business Page
- Facebook Ad Account
- Facebook App credentials
- Administrator access to connect

**Business Benefits**:
- **Speed**: Contact leads while interest is hot
- **Accuracy**: No transcription errors
- **Efficiency**: Salesperson focused on selling, not data entry
- **Insights**: Track which ads generate quality leads
- **Scalability**: Handle high volume campaigns easily

**Real-World Example**:
You run a Facebook ad campaign offering a free demo. Customer sees ad, clicks, fills form. Within 30 seconds:
- Lead in iPerform ✓
- Assigned to available salesperson ✓
- Salesperson notified on phone ✓
- Can call customer immediately ✓
- Customer impressed by fast response ✓

### 2. WhatsApp Business API Integration

**What It Does**:
Send WhatsApp messages to customers directly from iPerform for business communication.

**How It Works**:
1. You write message in iPerform
2. System sends via WhatsApp Business API
3. Customer receives on WhatsApp
4. Delivery confirmed
5. Responses tracked in iPerform

**Communication Types**:
- **One-to-One Messages**: Personal conversations
- **Bulk Campaigns**: Send to many customers
- **Automated Notifications**: Order updates, reminders
- **Rich Media**: Images, PDFs, location
- **Template Messages**: Pre-approved formats

**Important WhatsApp Rules**:
- Must use **approved templates** for campaigns
- Can't send random promotional messages
- Need customer opt-in
- Follow WhatsApp commerce policies
- Business hours only for broadcasts

**Business Benefits**:
- **High Open Rates**: 98% of WhatsApp messages opened
- **Customer Preference**: People prefer WhatsApp over email
- **Immediate Delivery**: Instant message arrival
- **Rich Features**: Share catalogs, PDFs, images
- **Global Reach**: WhatsApp used worldwide

**Use Cases**:
- Appointment reminders
- Demo confirmations
- Proposal delivery
- Follow-up messages
- Customer support
- Order status updates

**Setup Requirements**:
- WhatsApp Business API account (Meta)
- Verified business phone number
- Business documentation
- Message template approvals

## How Integrations Help Your Business

### Automation Example: Facebook Lead to First Call

**Without Integration** (Manual Process):
1. Someone fills Facebook form → 5 minutes later
2. Admin checks Facebook → 10 minutes later
3. Copies information to Excel → 5 minutes later
4. Emails salesperson → 10 minutes later
5. Salesperson enters in CRM → 5 minutes later
6. Finally calls customer → **35+ minutes total**

**With Integration** (Automated):
1. Someone fills Facebook form → Instant
2. Lead in iPerform automatically → 5 seconds
3. Assigned to salesperson → Instant
4. Notification sent → Instant
5. Salesperson calls → **Under 1 minute total**

**Impact**: 35x faster response, better conversion rate, happier customers!

## Troubleshooting Integrations

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Facebook Leads Not Coming**:
- Check Facebook connection status
- Verify app permissions
- Confirm webhook configuration
- Test with sample lead

**WhatsApp Messages Not Sending**:
- Verify WhatsApp Business API status
- Check template approval status
- Confirm phone number verified
- Review message limits

**Emails Going to Spam**:
- Check email authentication (SPF, DKIM)
- Review email content
- Monitor sending reputation
- Clean recipient list

**Files Not Uploading**:
- Check storage quota
- Verify file size limits
- Confirm cloud connection
- Test with smaller file
